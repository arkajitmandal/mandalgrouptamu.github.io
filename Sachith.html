Sachith is a Postdoctoral Researcher, working with Arkajit at the Department of Chemistry, Texas A&M University. His present research focuses on understanding cavity modified chemical and physical phenomena in molecules coupled to confined radiation mode. Sachith research interest spans a broad array of subjects including ab-initio molecular dynamics, nonadiabatic dynamics and machine learning. 

Sachith did his Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Sophya Garashchuk at the University of South Carolina. During his Ph.D. Sachith worked on simulating quantum dynamics using trajectory-guided adaptic basis and investigated hydroxyl transport in water using ab-initio molecular dynamics.
 
Sachith joined Texas A&M as a Postdoctoral Researcher in Sep, 2024.
